CGPA to Percentage Calculator (Anna University)

Convert your CGPA into percentage instantly using the official formula.

Formula: Percentage = (CGPA × 10) - 7.5

What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is a grading system used by universities in India to measure overall academic performance on a scale of 0 to 10.

How to Convert CGPA to Percentage?

For Anna University, the formula used is:

Percentage = (CGPA × 10) - 7.5

Example: If your CGPA is 8.5, then:

(8.5 × 10) - 7.5 = 77.5%

Why Convert CGPA to Percentage?

Many companies and universities prefer percentage instead of CGPA. Converting helps you present your academic performance clearly.

FAQs

Is 8 CGPA good?

Yes, a CGPA of 8 is considered above average and reflects strong academic performance.

Is CGPA same as percentage?

No, CGPA is a grade point average, while percentage is calculated marks.

Do all universities use same formula?

No, formulas vary. This tool is specific to Anna University.
